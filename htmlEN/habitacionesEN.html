Paradise room near the beach features a terrace and is located in Playa Honda, 7 min walk from Playa de Guacimeta and 1.6 km from Playa de la Concha.

The units are equipped with a patio, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with shower and hairdryer. There is also a microwave and refrigerator, as well as a coffee maker and kettle.

Rancho Texas Park is 8.2 km from the accommodation, and Lanzarote Golf Resort is 10 km away. The airport (Lanzarote Airport) is within walking distance.

Couples love the location - They've given it a 8,9 for two-person travel.
